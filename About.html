About PersuasiveDesign

This site is committed to presenting psychology and neuroscience concepts and their applications to making effective digital flows that benefit both the business and the user. This is a research and data-driven approach, and every concept will have solid, proven background studies on the topic and then move into practical examples of digital application. Where applicable, I'll include examples that I have seen proven out through personal experience and observation.

I’m Chris Medlin, a data-focused UX Certified Digital Product Manager and Certified Six Sigma Master Black Belt Process Engineer with a passion for using research, UX insights, and performance data to create digital experiences that actually matter to users.

With over 13 years of experience in digital product management — and more than 15 years in process engineering — across a range of sectors including Healthcare, auto loans, mortgages, credit cards, and consumer loans, I hope to use experiences of process improvement and digital transformation to help others.
