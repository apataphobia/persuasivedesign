Inattentional Blindness

Definition

Inattentional blindness is the psychological phenomenon where an individual fails to perceive an unexpected stimulus that is in plain sight.

Psychological Impact

Even highly noticeable elements can be missed if attention is focused elsewhere, affecting usability and safety.

Design Application

Minimize distractions and emphasize critical information with clear visual hierarchy and cues.
