Optimism Bias

Definition

Optimism bias refers to the cognitive bias that causes people to believe they are less likely to experience negative events compared to others.

Psychological Impact

Optimism bias can lead to risky behavior and underestimation of potential challenges.

Design Application

Frame content to gently highlight risks and consequences without being overly alarmist. Balance positivity with realism.
