Chunking

Definition

Chunking is the cognitive process of grouping information into smaller, manageable units to aid memory and processing.

Psychological Impact

Breaking information into chunks makes complex or large sets of data more digestible and easier to retain.

Design Application

Organize content into logical sections, steps, or groupings to reduce mental load and improve user navigation.
