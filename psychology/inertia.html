Inertia

Definition

Inertia bias refers to people's tendency to prefer maintaining the current state of affairs rather than making a change.

Psychological Impact

Users often avoid making decisions, even beneficial ones, to avoid effort or perceived risk.

Design Application

Make beneficial defaults easy to stick with, and reduce friction for positive change through guided actions.
