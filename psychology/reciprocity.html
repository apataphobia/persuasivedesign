Reciprocity

Definition

Reciprocity is the psychological principle where people feel obligated to return a favor or positive action.

Psychological Impact

Offering value upfront encourages users to reciprocate by completing a desired action, such as signing up or purchasing.

Design Application

Provide free value (e.g., free trials, resources) before asking for commitment or conversion.
